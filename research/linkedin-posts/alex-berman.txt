Action takers - They blast out GPT drafts just to get data.
 
And overthinkers - They pause, tweak the offer, target, and case studies to get everything perfect.
 
Most of the time in LIFE, I’m with the action takers. But this is the one place they’re wrong.
 
Here’s why:

Mid emails don’t convert.
