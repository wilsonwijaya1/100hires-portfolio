# Framework Comparison

| Framework | Core Structure | Best For | Length | Follow-up Strategy |
|-----------|----------------|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Connor 3-Body | Who you are → Priorities you solve → What you want (date ask) | High-volume SDR teams | 4–6 sentences | ABAB cadence, 24–48h |
| Alex Berman PC | Pinpoint pain → Call to action | Agency owners, consultants | Variable | 3–5 touches |
| Hoani Scale | Data-enriched fields + domain rotation | 10K+ emails/day | Short + mobile-first | Automated sequences |
| Taylor 2026 | Question subject + Loom drop | SaaS founders | Ultra-short | Video in follow-up #2 |
| YC Bootstrapper | Manual + phone + email combo | Limited budget | Longer, story-driven | 3 touches, phone-heavy |

---

## The #1 Mistake All Experts Agree On

**Spending too much time per email**. Connor says: 30–40 minutes per email in first 6 months = failure. The fix: batch everything upfront.

---

## The #1 Habit All Experts Agree On

**Track open rate, reply rate, meeting booked rate**. A 1–2% improvement in any of these compounds massively at volume.
