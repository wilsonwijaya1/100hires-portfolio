# Cold Outreach Quick Reference — Daily Execution

> One-page cheat sheet. Print this or keep it in your second monitor.

## Pre-Work (Once per week)
- [ ] Build 3–5 persona lists (50–200 contacts each)
- [ ] Write 1 template per persona
- [ ] Verify domains + inbox warm-up status

## Daily Rhythm
| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 8:00 AM | Send 50–100 emails (Monday list) |
| 10:00 AM | Call 30–50 from Tuesday list (leave voicemails) |
| 12:00 PM | Follow-up: Wednesday batch (copy-paste, 30 mins) |
| 2:00 PM | Call 30–50 from Thursday list |
| 4:00 PM | Follow-up: Friday batch |
| EOD | Update CRM: opens, replies, meetings booked |

## The 3-Body Rule
1. Who I am (1 sentence)
2. Why I'm reaching out (1 sentence priority + outcome)
3. What I want (specific time ask)

## Follow-up Limits
- Max 3 follow-ups after initial email
- 24–48h between touches
- Day 5: "Please give me your thoughts on this"
- Day 8: Assumptive breakup

## Metrics to Track Daily
- Emails sent: target 100+/day
- Open rate: target 20–25%
- Reply rate: target 5–10%
- Meetings from replies: target 15–20%

## What NOT to Do
- Overpersonalize (it doesn't scale)
- Use passive language ("if you're interested")
- Send >5 touches in a sequence
- Chase one prospect for >2 weeks
