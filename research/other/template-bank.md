# Template Bank

Derived from Alex Berman, Connor, Julian Goldie, and Laura Lopuch frameworks.

## Finance Persona (CFO / VP Finance / Controller)

```
Subject: Quick question about [Company]'s month-end process

Hi [FirstName],

My name's [YourName] and I'm part of [Product] financial applications team responsible for supporting [Company].

Given your role, I'm reaching out because we work with finance leaders who spend 10+ hours on manual month-end close. My team specifically focuses on priorities related to expense optimization, financial forecasting, and automated reporting.

I am looking to set some time for an introduction as my team will be the main point of contact for any priorities in these areas going forward.

What does your availability look like later this week?

Thanks in advance,
[YourName]
```

## Engineering Persona (CTO / VP Eng / Engineering Manager)

```
Subject: [Company] engineering stack question

Hi [FirstName],

I noticed [Company] is hiring 8 engineers on your careers page — sounds like you're scaling fast.

We work with engineering leaders at [comparable stage] to cut onboarding time for new devs by 40% without adding headcount. Our tool integrates directly with [GitHub/GitLab/Jira] and requires zero change management.

Are you open to a 15-minute intro this week or next?

Best,
[YourName]
```

## Marketing Persona (CMO / VP Marketing / Demand Gen)

```
Subject: [Company]'s Q[X] pipeline coverage

Hi [FirstName],

I saw [Company]'s recent campaign around [specific campaign] — solid work.

We help marketing leaders at B2B SaaS companies fill their pipeline with qualified opportunities using cold email + LinkedIn outbound. Our clients average 3–5 SQLs per week within 60 days.

Are you actively looking at outbound channels for Q[X]?

[YourName]
```

## Executive Persona (CEO / Founder)

```
Subject: [Company] growth question

Hi [FirstName],

Quick context: I help B2B SaaS founders add predictable pipeline without raising next round.

We work with companies at your stage (ARR, team size) to build a cold outreach channel that books 3–5 meetings/week within 30 days.

Is pipeline generation a focus for you right now?

[YourName]
```

---

## Rules
1. Change only [FirstName], [Company], and 1 specific relevant detail
2. Keep body identical across similar personas
3. Always propose specific dates, never "if you're interested"
