## How to Use This Template

1. **Add rows** for each expert whose work you review.
2. Under **Expert**, name the practitioner and their role if available (e.g., "John Doe — Head of Growth at Acme").
3. Under **Platform**, choose the primary source: `LinkedIn`, `YouTube`, `Twitter/X`, `Blog`, etc.
4. **URL** should link to the specific post, video, or article (or profile if summarizing multiple pieces).
5. **Recent Content** is a short description of the piece you reviewed (e.g., "Thread on 3-step cold email frameworks for SaaS outbound").
6. **Annotation** is your note on why this expert is relevant (e.g., "Verified recent output — posted 5 cold outreach threads in past 30 days; practitioner, not just a content marketer").
7. Prioritize **practitioners with verifiable recent output** over one-time viral authors.

## Expert Selection Criteria

- Must be a B2B SaaS growth / outbound practitioner (doer, not just a teacher).
- Must have published relevant content within the last 60 days.
- Prefer LinkedIn posts or YouTube transcripts for this project (see `linkedin-posts/` and `youtube-transcripts/` folders for raw captured content).
