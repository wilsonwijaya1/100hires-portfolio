## How to Use This File
- **Platform** = primary channel where the expert publishes cold-outreach content.
- **Recent Content** = title of the piece reviewed; dated within the last 60 days where possible.
- **Source File** = local file in this repo containing the raw transcript or post text.

## Expert Selection Criteria
1. **Practitioner-first** — selected people who actually run outbound motions (founders, SDR leaders, agency owners), not just course sellers.
2. **Recency** — content reviewed is within the last 60–90 days.
3. **Depth** — long-form videos (8–37 min) or structured LinkedIn articles preferred over 30-second clips.
4. **Verifiability** — every URL leads to a real, public profile or video.

## Raw Collections
- `youtube-transcripts/` — full video transcripts (English, auto-generated where manually created was unavailable).
- `linkedin-posts/` — public post text scraped directly from LinkedIn.
- `other/` — any supplementary frameworks, swipe files, or tool breakdowns.
